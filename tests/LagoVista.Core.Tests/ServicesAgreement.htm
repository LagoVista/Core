WHEREAS, Software Logistics provides software consulting and other services.

1. Services to be performed.

Software Logistics will provide proficient consultants proficient to perform services at Company's direction on projects as mutually agreed to in writing by Software Logistics and Company. All activities of the Software Logistics staff must be coordinated with the project supervisor of the Company. Company is responsible for making the necessary internal arrangements for the carrying out of the services to be rendered by the Software Logistics staff. Company and Software Logistics will jointly agree on the services for the project prior to the commencement of services in a writing to be attached to and forming part of this Agreement ("Attachment").

2. Satisfaction with performance.

If at any time Company is dissatisfied with the performance of a consultant working on a Company project, Company shall immediately report its dissatisfaction to Software Logistics in writing and may ask Software Logistics to replace the individual.

3. Compensation to Software Logistics.

Services will be provided by Software Logistics on a time and materials or a fixed bid contract basis depending on the Attachment. Time & Material activities will be charged according to the rates as set forth in Software Logistics? then current List of Prices. The services to be rendered by Software Logistics within the framework of this Agreement will be charged to the client twice each month in accordance with Software Logistics? current List of Prices. Travel time (departure airport to client destination) will be billed at ? rate of the rate of the consultant set forth in Software Logistics' current List of Prices.? Any overtime hours (beyond 8 hours per day or 40 hours per week) will be billed at 1.5 times the rates set forth in Software Logistics? current List of Prices. Record of performance will be shown on forms provided by Software Logistics for this purpose. All payments are due and payable upon receipt of invoice. All duties, taxes and levies (excluding taxes based on Software Logistics? net income), if any, shall be borne by Company. Software Logistics' consulting rates are subject to change upon thirty (30) days prior written notice. Company shall pay a one and one half percent penalty per month retroactive to the invoice date for payment(s) received after thirty (30) days. Company shall be responsible for all reasonable and documented expenses incurred by Software Logistics, including out-of-pocket expenses, at Software Logistics? cost plus 10 percent in providing services under this Agreement.
